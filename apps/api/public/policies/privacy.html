VillKro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-17

1. Who we are

VillKro is a hyperlocal marketplace app built and operated by Yash Siwach, based in Gorakhpur, Haryana 125047, India. Contact: decodelabsofficial@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

  • Account: name, phone, bcrypt-hashed password, optional WhatsApp number.
  • Delivery addresses you save.
  • Approximate location (city / pin code) during checkout, foreground only.
  • Expo push token for order status notifications.
  • Order history: items, vendor, status, timestamps.

3. Why we collect it

Account management, order delivery, push notifications, fraud prevention.

4. Third parties we work with

  • Azure MySQL — account data, delivery addresses, sessions.
  • MongoDB Atlas — order history (items, vendor, status, timestamps).
  • AWS S3 — product photos uploaded by vendors.
  • Expo / Google FCM — push notifications (receives your device push token).

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers.

5. Retention

Active account + 30 days after deletion.

6. Your rights

  • Access: email decodelabsofficial@gmail.com
  • Correction: in the app, Profile → Edit Profile
  • Deletion: in the app, Profile → Delete Account (immediate soft-delete, hard-purged in 30 days)
  • Withdraw consent: uninstall the app and email us

7. Children

VillKro is for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

8. Security

All traffic is over HTTPS. Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. Production database credentials are restricted to the application server.

9. Changes to this policy

The "Last updated" date at the top will change when this policy changes. Material changes will also be shown inside the app.
