VillKro — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 2026-06-19

1. Two ways to delete your VillKro account

A. Self-service from the app (instant)

  1. Open the VillKro app and log in.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom right.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap Delete account.
  4. Read the confirmation screen, then tap Delete permanently.

Your account, personal data, addresses, cart, and order history are deleted immediately. Active orders in progress are cancelled automatically.

B. Request by email (within 30 days)

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request from the email address registered on your VillKro account to:

decodelabsofficial@gmail.com

Subject: Delete my VillKro account

Include your registered phone number in the email body so we can verify the request. We will confirm and complete the deletion within 30 days.

2. What data is deleted

  • Account profile: name, phone, email (if provided), password hash.
  • Delivery addresses you saved.
  • Cart contents.
  • Authentication tokens and active sessions.
  • Push notification tokens.
  • Order history (orders placed by you).

3. What data may be retained

  • Financial records: If you completed a paid order, the transaction record is retained for up to 8 years as required by Indian tax law (Income Tax Act, 1961).
  • Anonymised analytics: Aggregated, non-identifying usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.
  • Backups: Backups of deleted data are purged within 90 days as part of our normal backup rotation.

4. After deletion

  • You will not be able to recover the account or any data once deletion is complete.
  • If you only want to stop using the app temporarily, you can simply log out — your data stays intact.

5. Contact

For any questions about this deletion policy, email decodelabsofficial@gmail.com.
